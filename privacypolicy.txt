Privacy Policy
Last updated: 11 August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you sign up for my newsletter.

1. Information I Collect
When you subscribe to my newsletter, I collect your email address. 
I do not collect any other personal information.

2. How I Use Your Information
Your email address will only be used to send you updates about my artwork, exhibitions, writings, and related news.
I will never sell, rent, or share your email address with anyone.

3. How I Store Your Information
Your email address is stored securely and accessed only by me through an encrypted environment provided by an open-source Cryptpad project. (https://cryptpad.fr).

4. Your Rights
You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time by requesting to have your email removed from my records. 
Contact me directly at contact@majabraumberger.com

5. Analytics
This website uses GoatCounter to understand visitor traffic. 
This tool does not collect personally identifiable information.
